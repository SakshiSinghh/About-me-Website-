Science And Research

My friends and I decided to team up because prior to this competition we are involved as a science research team in the HCI science research programme to investigate piezoelectricity. I decided to take part in the Singapore Energy Grand challenge because it was an opportunity for me to involve my critical thinking .Due, to the increased use of non renewable energy such as fossil fuels , we decided that our previous research idea and other sustainable energy methods could be explored in real world contexts such as our school.Firstly , we brainstormed with our teacher on feasible and sustainable methods which we could install in our school through the minecraft world. We collaborated on our ideas and further enhanced it . Secondly , we attended the clinical sessions which made us more aware of the hurdles Singapore faces which allowed us to further scrutinize our suggested ideas . Thus through this process , we decided on energy efficient methods that could be used for a prolonged period of time and for the future . We were very happy and felt that all of our long hours of hard work was worth it .
